A Safe Space to Be

Sometimes healing happens not through words, but through returning to the wisdom of our bodies. Sometimes we need to speak what we've carried. Often, we need both.

I offer individualized yoga sessions for those navigating complex PTSD recovery. This work honors wherever you are in your process—the storms, the stillness, and everything in between.

How We Work Together

Our sessions create space for your nervous system to find its own rhythm. We work with breath, gentle movement, and grounding practices that help you reconnect with your body's innate capacity for healing.

I understand that trauma recovery is not linear. Some days you may need to move, others to be still. Some sessions may bring up feelings that need expression. I am comfortable being present with whatever arises—your protective mechanisms, your pain, your resilience—all of it is welcome.

The practice often helps quiet the mind's protective chatter and guides us back into embodied presence, but we honor whatever unfolds in each moment.

Important to Know

I am not a licensed therapist, counselor, or mental health professional. What I offer is trauma-informed yoga support that complements—but does not replace—professional therapeutic care.

I deeply respect the therapeutic process and understand that healing often requires both body-based practices and professional mental health support. I encourage working with qualified therapists alongside our yoga practice.
